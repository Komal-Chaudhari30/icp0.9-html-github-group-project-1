Tulip Garden

History : The Tulip Garden opened in 2007.

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit the Tulip Garden in Srinagar is the month of April, when the tulips are in full bloom and the annual Tulip Festival is held. Though, this popular attraction in Srinagar is open throughout the year for the visitors.

Visiting Timings: Tulip Garden opens from 9:00 am to 7:00 pm every day.

Entry Fee: The entry fee for Tulip Garden is INR 50 for adults and INR 25 for children.

Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip garden, previously Model Floriculture Center, is a tulip garden in Srinagar. It is the largest tulip garden in Asia spread over an area of about 30 hectares. It is situated on the foothills of Zabarwan Range with an overview of Dal Lake. The garden was opened in 2007 with the aim to boost floriculture and tourism in Kashmir Valley. The garden is built on a sloping ground in a terraced fashion consisting of seven terraces.
